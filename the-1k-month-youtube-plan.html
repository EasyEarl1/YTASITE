03 - Niche selection
Your niche
is your salary.

A $40 RPM niche earns 40x more per 1,000 views than a $1 RPM niche. Same effort. Same video count. Completely different outcome. Use the HEAT framework to pick the right one:

H
High-Value Customer
Target people who spend money. A Shopify store owner is worth far more to advertisers than a casual gamer.
E
Evergreen Content
Pick topics relevant for years. "How to set up Shopify payments" will be searched in 5 years. A trend won't.
A
Actionable Content
Niches where viewers buy things: software, finance, legal, tools. This keeps RPM high and adds affiliate income.
T
Trouble-Free Content
Avoid risky topics. Gambling, adult content, and sensitive areas can get your channel demonetized.
Proven starting niches

Shopify tutorials, QuickBooks, software how-tos, real estate basics, tax and accounting. All $30–$60 RPMs. All have thousands of unanswered keywords.

06 - The failure points
Why most people
never hit $1k/month

This model works. The math is real. So why do most people who try it never see results? Always the same four mistakes.

✕
Wrong keywords

Too broad, too competitive, low RPM. "Make money online" is not a keyword. "How to set up Shopify payments in Australia" is. The difference in results is massive.

✕
No consistency

Upload 10 videos, see nothing in week one, quit. This model requires volume over time. The channel that uploads 1,000 wins. The one that stops at 30 earns nothing.

✕
Bad execution

Videos that don't actually answer the search. If it takes 8 minutes to get to the point, they leave. Retention dies. Rankings die. Income dies.

✕
No system

Everything manual, run out of ideas, burn out, stop. Without a keyword bank and production process this stays a hobby that earns nothing.

The honest truth

Most people get stuck not because the model doesn't work - but because they try to figure it out alone, without the right tools. That's exactly where we come in.
